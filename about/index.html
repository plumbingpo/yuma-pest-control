How We Got Started

We started Yuma Pest Control with one goal: to give Yuma residents pest control they can rely on. What started as a small family crew with a truck and a passion for protecting homes has grown into a full-service pest control company, but our core values have never changed.

Our philosophy is simple: get it right, be respectful, and always be upfront about costs. Our team of experienced technicians brings decades of combined experience to every job, from routine ant and spider inspections to large-scale termite and rodent treatments.

Being locally owned means we're invested in Yuma and the people who live here. We live here, we work here, and we're committed to serving our neighbors with the highest level of professionalism and care.
